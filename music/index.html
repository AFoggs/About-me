Inspiration

Skylar Grey

Skylar Grey is my favorite vocalist of all the musicians I listen to. Talented with both her vocals and voice in the shape of songwriting, she is an awe-inspiring musician. Many people don't know it, but skylar grey co-wrote and featured in many popular songs enjoyed by many. One that surprises people the most is that she is the one who co-wrote "Love the way you lie" (Sung by Rihanna. In addition to that, you may have heard her voice in Dr.Dre's "I need a Doctor," Diddy's "Coming home," and Lupe Fiasco's "Words I Never said." Not only a vocalist, Skylar is a wonderful musician well versed in Piano and Guitar. She is one of my main inspirations for teaching myself how to play piano and guitar.
